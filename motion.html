BERRI INTRO

I found Berri's design to double as a character design and logo, similar to how Hello Kityy usually represents Sanrio. As if it was the introductory start of a video, Berri hops around before other elements of the design appear on the canvas. The smoothness of the transitions were the main focus of making it look neat and professional.

RETRO TV

This assignment had us flicker between things on the TV screen. I used some of my personal art as well as images to give the feeling of being at a concert.
