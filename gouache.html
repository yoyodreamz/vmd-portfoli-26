FAUVISM PAINTING

Before starting the painting, I took a trip to the Botanical Gardens in San Francisco, photographing flowers in bloom. A specific area with a tree and flowers made the most interesting potential for composition. I created a digital mockup og the image, drawing the figure and altering the colors of the image to appear more fauvist before tracing the design on bristol and painting it in gouache.

DECONSTRUCTED COLOR WHEEL

For this assignment, I had to use a tint, tone, shade, and pure hue of 12 hues in a painting. With freedom of creativity, I decided to paint 4 of my character and incorporate the colors in their designs.
