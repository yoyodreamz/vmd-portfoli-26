BERRI

The style of Berri was inspired by merchandise and entertainment companies like Sanrio and San-X. While working at Build-a-Bear Workshop and observing the stuffed animal market, there was a desire amongst a lot of people for a simple black bear. Berri was created as a concept to fill that niche. On top of being modeled from American black bears, she has very subtle inclusions of the blackberry fruit in her shape language, giving her a fluffier appearance. Since fruit is also a part of their diet, that is where she gets her name from, as well as the inspiration for the type of flower she wears.

ICON

For this assignment, I needed to create an icon that represented me. The design of the icon was less important than the color choices it was used for, but it needed to be a vector. On top of the 2000's cartoon style, I wanted to make look vaguely alien.

OP ART

The task for this assignment was to give the illution of depth and motion with solid color (no blending/shading or gradients). Illustrator was chosen to both deter the use of unallowed elements and for easy scalability.
