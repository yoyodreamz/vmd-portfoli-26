SELF-PORTRAIT

Saturated colors and bold graphics are two of my favorite design choices. Growing up in the late 2000s to 2010s, I was exposed to online pop culture and Frutiger aesthetics. It also wasn't uncommon to see graphics like this in adolescent-marketed media. Combining all of these things I love brought me to this design.

METAPHORICAL PORTRAIT

The illustration was not allowed to resemble the subject, instead opting for a symbolic representation. An interview was conducted, and I brainstormed a composition with a figure as the placeholder. A character design was created using the interview as inspiration, and replaced the figure before final details were added.

COLOR EXPLORATION - 1

After creating a character design, I was tasked to use color in a way that invoked a sense of protectiveness, vigilance, and bravery in a character. I believe #8 is the most successful, but its interesting to see which ones appeal to people the most!
